Steps to add signature in Outlook:

  1. Open Outlook and click on File > Options
  2. Select Mail from the left sidebar
  3. Scroll down to the Signatures section and click Signatures...
  4. Click New to create a new signature
  5. Give your signature a name and click OK
  6. In the signature editor, click the HTML button ()
  7. Paste the copied signature HTML
  8. Click OK to save
  9. Choose when to use the signature in the dropdown menus below
  10. Click OK to finish
